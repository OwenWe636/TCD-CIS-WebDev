Whats Minecraft About?

Minecraft is a voxel-based sandbox first developed by Markus Person in 2009 as an indie game, and released to the public in 2011. The game is currently owned by Microsoft, and has stood the test of time as one of the most popular games of all time, and paved the way for creativity in many forms, one of them being mods. Minecraft is a storytelling medium by design, and can be modified to one's own arm span, and that's one of the many reasons why I fell in love with it. Ever since I was little, I would watch videos about minecraft mods from popular youtubers such as DanTDM, PopularMMOS, and many more. As I grew older, and fazed out of previous interests, Minecraft only grew stronger in my mind as something I couldn’t go without. With the yearly update adding more content, to new and creative mods being made by creative people, there isn’t a true end to it, and I wouldn’t have it any other way. .

Why I like the game

I’m currently a mod artist for two mods, The Broken Script: Additions, and The Broken Script: Blackout, which are add-on mods to The Broken Script, a horror mod centered around a terrifying and heartbreaking story centered around the remnants of a lost server, the spirits of the players who once were, and the many horrors haunting the very code of the game I’m playing. I’ve looked up to this mod ever since I discovered it, and had managed to befriend other developers of which I can share my artistic talent with, and if this isn’t an example of finding my footing, I don’t know what is.
